About The Apple Store

Discover Innovation at IIT Patna's Premier Tech Hub Welcome to the cutting-edge on-campus destination for all things Apple, conveniently located at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna in Bihta. As the official nexus for technology and creativity within the campus community, we are dedicated to powering the next generation of engineers, innovators, and thinkers.

Our Mission:

To provide students, faculty, and staff with seamless access to Apple's revolutionary products and services, fostering a culture of technological excellence and innovation.

What We Offer:

  1. Apple Products: Explore the latest iPhones, MacBooks, iPads, and accessories designed to enhance your academic and personal life.
  2. Technical Support: Our knowledgeable staff is here to assist with troubleshooting, repairs, and technical advice to keep you connected.
  3. Workshops and Events: Participate in exclusive workshops, product launches, and events that inspire creativity and learning.
  4. Student Discounts: Enjoy special pricing on Apple products as a member of the IIT Patna community.
